One of my all time favorite restaurants! Dominican food that is delicious and reasonable. As soon and you walk in the decor makes you instantly feel at ease and relaxed. I've never ordered something that I didn't like from this restaurant!
My favorites on the menu?
Ceviche de Camarones - shrimp ceviche marinated in lemon juice $8
Pechuga a la Plancha - boneless grilled chicken breast with light garlic & parsley sauce $11
Platanos Maduros - fried sweet plantains $3
